insight

For anyone who wants to accel in any of these three sports, my advice to you is have a mindset that there are no limitations to your progression. You may not get first on your last cross country meet, or can't ride up the entire rattlesnake gulch without uncliping your pedals, or are struggling to learn a new freestyle skiing trick. This doesn't mean that you just arent made to acheive those goals. No matter how ambitious they may be, with the right amount of dedication and hardwork, anything is possible when you put your mind to it. Take baby steps, be patient with yourself, and don't give up.
